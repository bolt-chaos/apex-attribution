========================================================================
VERDICT
========================================================================
v1 FAILS the literature bar (car should dominate the modern era). Both ICC and the
interventional spreads put almost all explanatory weight on the DRIVER, because each
driver is nested in one constructor (selection confounding, pitfall #1) and the raw
categorical SCM cannot separate latent skill from latent car pace (pitfall #2). The
teammate structure was used only to pick a connected cohort, NOT to identify relative
skill — so the identifying lever is unused in the SCM itself.

DELIVERED: the required gcm machinery runs end-to-end (ICC, interventional/counterfactual
swaps, and the reliability-combined unified metric). DO NOT trust the v1 attribution
numbers. FIX (deferred, out of v1 scope): a hierarchical latent skill/pace model that
estimates constructor-level car pace and driver-level skill as separate parameters
(identified via the teammate contrasts in [5]) and feeds those into the SCM.
